About IEEE Kochi Hub

IEEE Kochi hub has always been a hive of highly passionate and enthusiastic volunteers who always strive to come up with groundbreaking ideas and events.

We have a great legacy of upbringing a bunch of high-spirited and brightest minds who are keen to work towards advancing the technology for humanity, thus serving the very purpose of IEEE. In Kochi hub we 'aspire to inspire those young minds to spark against all the odds!!'

About Kochi Hub Meet

IEEE Kochi hub meet is the premier event of Kochi hub where one could witness the best of workshops and sessions with a chance to volunteer and network with like minded IEEEians in full zest and zeal!

KHM offers a great platform for the ones whose quest to be in pace with the advancing technology is always high and with that an opportunity to interact with our 36 SBs across 5 zones.
